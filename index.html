Yuying Tang 唐誉瑛

I'm a PhD student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, working under the guidance of Professor Huamin Qu and Associate Professor Xiaojun Ma. My research focuses on Human-Computer Interaction, Human-AI Interaction, and Visualization. I hold a Master of Fine Art in Information Art and Design from Tsinghua University (China), a Master of Science in Communication Design from the Polytechnic University of Milan (Italy), and a Bachelor of Fine Art in Film and Television Photography and Production from the Central Academy of Fine Arts (China). My current research interests lie in AI storytelling and AI integration with design.
