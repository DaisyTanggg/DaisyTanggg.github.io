Yuying Tang 唐誉瑛

Yuying Tang is a PhD student and researcher at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Human-AI Interaction (HAI), and Visualization. She holds degrees from Tsinghua University, the Polytechnic University of Milan, and the Central Academy of Fine Arts. Yuying is committed to advancing user-centered AI systems that enhance everyday experiences through her research.
